Blossom

The self-proclaimed leader of the Powerpuff Girls.Her unique power is freezing objects with her breath as seen in the episode "Ice Sore".

Buttercup

A "tough hotheaded tomboy". Buttercup is the only Powerpuff Girl without a unique super power.

Bubbles

The "softest and sweetest" of the three. Her unique power is emitting supersonic waves with her voice.
